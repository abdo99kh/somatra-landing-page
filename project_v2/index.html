About Us

Somatra for Cleaning Products Import is a Libya-based company headquartered in Tripoli, specializing in the import and distribution of high-quality cleaning products for both household and commercial use.

At Somatra, we are committed to excellence, efficiency, and customer satisfaction. Through strong logistics management and a growing distribution network across Libya, we aim to provide dependable products that support healthier homes, cleaner workplaces, and sustainable business growth.

Mission

To supply high-quality cleaning products that improve everyday hygiene standards while providing reliable service, competitive pricing, and consistent product availability to our customers across Libya.

Vision

To become one of Libya’s leading companies in the import and distribution of cleaning and hygiene products, recognized for quality, efficiency, and innovation.
